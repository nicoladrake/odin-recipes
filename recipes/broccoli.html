Broccoli

Description

What a delicious vegetable! It's so good for you. Pairs well with the bulgogi you would have made.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli (1)
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Baste the broccoli with oil, salt and pepper.
  2. Bake in the oven for twenty minutes at 1000 degrees.
  3. Enjoy!
